Return Policy

Returns & Exchanges Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with a Perfume Land purchase or gift for any reason, please review our return, refund, and exchange policies below for online purchases (lk.perfumeland.com) and in-store purchases.

Products

Products must be returned in new or gently used condition in their original packaging with proof of purchase. If in a set, all products and packaging in that set need to be returned with not more than one item in the gift pack in a gently used condition, with all others being in an unused & unopened condition.

All product must be in its original packaging (container plus box if applicable) and should be unmarked and not broken or defaced in any manner.

Perfume Land monitors return activity for abuse and reserves the right to limit returns or exchanges at Perfume Land.

Loyalty Rewards

Loyalty rewards associated with all refunds will be removed when the refund is processed.

Free Products

Free products (if any) with purchases cannot be returned/exchanged.

Special offer or discount given at the time of purchase will be adjusted when the refund is processed.

Gift certificates/Vouchers cannot be returned/exchanged.

All returns are subject to validation and approval at Perfume Land's discretion.

THIS POLICY IS EFFECTIVE FOR ALL PURCHASES MADE ON AND AFTER 1st March 2021.

Online Purchases (lk.perfumeland.com)

Online purchases (lk.perfumeland.com) can be easily returned to any of our Perfume Land outlets for fast processing, or by courier if using our return shipping label included in your online order boxes (follow the return procedures described below).

We regret we are unable to refund your original shipping charges, if applicable, unless the return was a result of our error.

Items purchased online at lk.perfumeland.com may not be returned and will not be refunded after 21 days from the purchase date.

Please allow 3-14 working days for your return to be processed if returned via our courier.

When you return your online purchase by courier:

Please send email to returns@perfumeland.com to inform us of the product return. Following information must be in your email: Name of purchaser, date of purchase, invoice number, amount on invoice, Names and number of item/s being returned, reason for return.

Product replacements and refunds will be processed after we receive the product at our manufacturing facility.

Items mailed/posted to Perfume Land outside of the returns window, not in new or gently used condition, without its original Perfume Land packaging and without proof of purchase, will not be credited back or returned to you.
