Product Details

Chanel No. 5 is a timeless classic fragrance that has been captivating women for almost a century. It is a floral aldehyde perfume that is elegant, feminine, and classy. The top notes of bergamot, lemon, and neroli are fresh and citrusy, while the middle notes of jasmine, rose, and ylang-ylang are sensual and romantic.

The base notes of musk, vanilla, and sandalwood give the fragrance warmth and depth, making it both luxurious and comforting.

The fragrance of Chanel No. 5 is rich and decadent, with a powdery softness that surrounds the wearer in a cloud of pure class. It is the ideal scent for special events and nights out since it radiates elegance and sophistication. The recognizable square bottle has a simple label.
