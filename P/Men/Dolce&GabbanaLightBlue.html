Product Details

Dolce & Gabbana Light Blue is a captivating and refreshing fragrance that evokes the essence of the Mediterranean. Its crisp and invigorating scent is perfect for those who love to feel energized and alive. It is a versatile scent that is both traditional and modern, making it ideal for any setting.

This scent's top notes, which combine tart Sicilian lemon and luscious apple, give off a rush of freshness. The fragrance's heart notes, which combine fragrant rosemary and delicate jasmine petals, give it a hint of flowery elegance. Warm amber and musk make up the fragrance's base notes.
